Features

Everything you need on site, nothing you don't.

Three screens, one goal: a clean quote before you've even left the client.

01 — Quote

A complete quote in minutes.

Client, zones to tile, floor and wall areas, materials — every field built to be filled with one thumb, on site, without wifi.

  • Built-in area calculator (length × width)
  • Automatic, configurable sand/cement calculation
  • Professional PDF export in one tap
02 — Express Mode

One text, one complete quote.

Describe the job the way you'd jot it in a notebook. DevisCarrelage pulls out zones, areas and rates automatically — exactly like the demo above.

  • Free-form, natural language input
  • Quick examples to get started instantly
  • AI fallback available for complex descriptions (Pro)
03 — History

Your history, always organized.

Every quote, found in an instant. Edit it, regenerate the PDF, or delete it — without ever losing track of a job.

  • Instant search by client
  • Amounts and areas visible at a glance
  • Drafts saved automatically
